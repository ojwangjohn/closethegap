Meet Close the Gap

We bridge the digital divide with the Circular Economy HUB and the Close the Gap HUB

Close the Gap RSA is a social enterprise that aims to bridge the digital divide by offering high-quality, pre-owned computers collected from European and international companies to educational, medical, and social projects in developing and emerging countries.We bridge the digital divide with the Circular Economy HUB and the Close the Gap HUB, where we innovate, educate, and manufacture

The Circular Economy HUB is our state of art IT asset disposition facility where we assemble computer components, mobile devices, spare parts, and other tech devices into nearly-new ‘made in RSA’ devices. These devices will be used in educational, medical, and social projects. The Circular Economy HUB is situated in Philippi Village, Cwangco Crescent, Philippi 7781 Cape Town.

The Close the Gap HUB is our one-stop shop. It is the dynamic heart of individual and international co-workers, young entrepreneurs, and start-ups. It’s an inspirational and specious co-working space with meeting rooms to host all your meetings and events. Close the Gap HUB is strategically situated Philippi Village, Cwangco Crescent, Philippi 7781 Cape Town.
